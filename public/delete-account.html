Delete Your Account

Zanbi, operated by Embra LLC

You can permanently delete your Zanbi account and its associated data at any time. There are two ways to do it — pick whichever is easier for you.

Option 1 — In the app (fastest)

  1. Open the Zanbi app and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.

Your account is removed right away, subject to the limited retention described below.

Option 2 — From the web (no app needed)

If you can't access the app, request deletion by email and we'll process it for you. Tap the button below — it opens a pre-filled message. Just add your account email and send.

Request account deletion by email

Or email support@embrallc.com directly with the subject "Delete my Zanbi account" and include the email address on your account so we can verify you. We'll confirm once it's done.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes your account profile and the inspection data associated with it — including your inspections, custom forms, photos, notes, and reports stored in Zanbi.

What we may keep, and why. We retain a limited set of records where the law or a provider requires it — for example, payment and transaction records held by Stripe for financial-compliance reasons, and minimal records needed for security, tax, or fraud prevention. These are kept only as long as required and are not used to re-create your account. Backups are purged on a rolling schedule.

Organization accounts: if you're the owner of a team/organization, tell us in your request whether you want just your own membership removed or the entire organization and its shared inspection data deleted, so we handle your team's records correctly.

How long it takes

We complete deletion requests within the period required by applicable law (typically within 30 days), and usually much sooner. If you email us, we'll send a confirmation when your data has been removed.
