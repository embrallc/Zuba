Privacy Policy

Zanbi, operated by Embra LLC  •  Effective: 06-21-2026  •  Last updated: 6/21

This Privacy Policy explains how Embra LLC ("Embra," "we," "us," or "our"), the operator of the Zanbi mobile application and the website at getzanbi.com (together, the "Service"), collects, uses, shares, and protects information. By creating an account or using the Service, you agree to this Policy.

Our two roles. Zanbi is a tool for professional home inspectors. (1) For information about you, the inspector / account holder, Embra is the data controller. (2) For information you enter about your own customers and the properties you inspect (e.g., a homebuyer's name, phone, address, inspection photos, and report), Embra acts as a service provider / data processor that handles that data on your behalf and under your instructions. As the inspector, you are responsible for having a lawful basis and any required consent to collect that data and to have us send reports, invoices, and text/email reminders to your customers and their designated recipients.

1. Information we collect

1.1 Information you provide (inspector account)

  • Identity & account: name, email address, password (stored only as a salted hash), business/organization name, team role, and any profile details you add.
  • Payment onboarding: when you connect a payout account to bill your customers, identity and bank details you provide go directly to Stripe; we receive only your connected-account status and metadata (we never receive or store full card or bank numbers).
  • Subscription: your plan and entitlement status, managed through RevenueCat and the Apple App Store / Google Play (we do not receive your card number).
  • Support & communications: messages, feedback, and information you send us.

1.2 Information about your customers (entered by you, the inspector)

When you schedule and perform inspections, you may enter and we will process on your behalf:

  • Customer contact details: full name, phone number, email address(es), and the email addresses of additional report/invoice recipients you designate (e.g., a real-estate agent).
  • Property & location: property address and its geographic coordinates (latitude/longitude).
  • Inspection content: notes, custom form responses, severity ratings, photographs of the property, voice notes, and the generated inspection report.

1.3 Information collected automatically

  • Device & app data: device model, operating system, app version, language/region, and unique device or installation identifiers.
  • Usage & diagnostics: feature usage, crash reports, and error logs (which may include timestamps and limited technical context) used to operate and improve the Service.
  • Push tokens: a token used to deliver notifications you enable.
  • Server log & network data: IP address and request metadata captured by our infrastructure providers for security and reliability.

1.4 Precise location

With your permission, we collect your device's precise location to optimize your daily driving route, estimate arrival times and drive time/mileage, and detect when you have arrived at an inspection. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings; route features will be limited without it.

1.5 Payment information

Card payments your customers make are processed by Stripe, which is responsible for handling cardholder data in accordance with PCI-DSS. We store only a non-sensitive mirror of payment status (e.g., requested, paid, refunded), the payment amount, and Stripe identifiers — never full card numbers.

2. How we use information

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and secure the Service.
  • Schedule inspections; optimize routes; compute arrival times, drive time, and mileage.
  • Generate, store, and deliver inspection reports to recipients you designate.
  • Facilitate payments from your customers to you, and collect our platform fee.
  • Send appointment reminders, reports, invoices, and service notifications you or your customers have opted into.
  • Provide AI-assisted features (see §3).
  • Manage your account, subscription, and provide customer support.
  • Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud, abuse, and security incidents.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.
  • Analyze and improve the Service (using aggregated or de-identified data where feasible).

Where required (e.g., EEA/UK), our legal bases are: performance of our contract with you; your consent (e.g., location, marketing, SMS); our legitimate interests in operating and securing the Service; and compliance with legal obligations.

3. AI & voice features

  • AI report assistance. When you use AI features (e.g., turning rough notes into report prose, or a daily route briefing), the relevant text is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a suggestion. Per Google's API terms, content submitted through the paid Gemini API is not used to train Google's models. AI output may contain errors — you are responsible for reviewing and approving all report content before it is delivered. AI suggestions are not professional, legal, or engineering advice.
  • Voice-to-text. If you dictate notes, audio is transcribed using your device's operating-system speech-recognition capability, which may process audio on-device or via the platform provider (Apple or Google) under their privacy policies.

4. How we share information

We share information only as described here:

  • Sub-processors / service providers. With the vendors listed in §5, who process data on our behalf under contract.
  • At your direction. Reports, invoices, payment links, and reminders are sent to the customers and additional recipients you designate for each inspection.
  • Within your organization. Inspection data is shared among members of your business/team account according to their roles.
  • Legal & safety. When required by law, subpoena, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the public, or Embra.
  • Business transfers. In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to this Policy.
We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. Mobile information (your phone number and SMS consent) is never shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes, and text-messaging opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third party except sub-processors strictly necessary to deliver the messages you requested (e.g., our SMS carrier).

6. Device permissions

The app requests these permissions; each is optional and used only for the stated purpose:

  • Location — route optimization, ETAs, mileage, arrival detection.
  • Camera — capture inspection photos.
  • Photo library / media — attach photos to inspections and save generated reports.
  • Microphone — dictate voice notes (speech-to-text).
  • Calendar — sync inspection appointments to your device calendar.
  • Notifications — appointment and reminder alerts you enable.

7. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the Service, then delete or de-identify it within a commercially reasonable period, unless a longer period is required for legal, accounting, security, fraud-prevention, or dispute-resolution purposes. Inspection records you create are retained so you can access your business history; you can delete individual records, and deleting your account removes your data as described in §10. Backups are purged on a rolling schedule.

8. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, row-level database access controls, scoped API keys held only on our servers, and secure on-device storage of credentials (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore). No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security, and you are responsible for safeguarding your login credentials.

9. Your rights & choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or update your personal information.
  • Delete your personal information (see §10).
  • Receive a portable copy of your data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, and withdraw consent (e.g., location, SMS, marketing).
  • Opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information — note that we do not sell or share it.
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights.

To exercise any right, email support@embrallc.com. We will verify and respond within the time required by applicable law. If we process customer data on behalf of an inspector, we will refer requests from that inspector's customers to the inspector and assist as the service provider. You may also manage notifications and permissions in your device settings, and reply STOP to any text message to opt out.

10. Account & data deletion

You can delete your account and associated personal data in two ways:

  • In the app: Settings → Account → Delete Account.
  • From the web / without the app: visit getzanbi.com/delete-account or email support@embrallc.com with the subject "Delete my account."

Deleting your account permanently removes your account profile and the inspection data associated with it, except where we must retain limited records for legal, tax, security, or fraud-prevention reasons (e.g., transaction records held by Stripe for financial compliance). We complete deletion requests within the period required by applicable law.

The Service is intended for professional/business use and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK). If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Message frequency varies; message and data rates may apply; reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help. As stated in §4, mobile opt-in and consent information is not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing, and is not sold. See our SMS Terms for details.
