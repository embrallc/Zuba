SMS Appointment Reminder — Consent & Opt-In

Program (brand): Zuba  •  Operated by: Embra LLC  •  Website: apps.embrallc.com

1. Program overview

Zuba is a home-inspection scheduling app used by professional home inspectors. With the customer's consent, inspectors send a single appointment-reminder text message before a scheduled home inspection. These messages are transactional (service reminders tied to an appointment the customer booked) — they are not marketing or promotional.

Use case: Appointment reminders
Message frequency: Low — typically 1–2 messages per inspection (a reminder before the appointment, plus any confirmation/HELP/STOP replies).
Who sends: The customer's home inspector, through Zuba.
Who receives: Customers who scheduled a home inspection and explicitly agreed to reminders.

2. How customers opt in

Consent is collected directly by the inspector at the time the inspection is scheduled (by phone, in person, or via a booking form). The customer provides their mobile number and gives explicit permission to receive reminder texts before the inspector enables reminders for that appointment. Consent is not a condition of service — a customer can decline reminders and still book their inspection. Customers can opt out at any time (see §5).

3. Opt-in script (what the inspector asks the customer)

Spoken / phone & in person
"As part of scheduling your home inspection, we can send a text reminder before your appointment. Would you like that? These are appointment reminders only — message frequency varies (usually 1–2 messages), message and data rates may apply, and you can reply STOP at any time to opt out or HELP for help. Your number is used only for these reminders and is never sold. Do I have your permission to text appointment reminders to this number?"
Written / booking form / checkbox
☐ Yes, text me appointment reminders. By checking this box and providing my mobile number, I agree to receive SMS appointment reminders from Zuba (operated by Embra LLC) at the number provided. Message frequency varies. Message & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to cancel, HELP for help. Consent is not a condition of purchase. See our Privacy Policy and SMS Terms.

4. Sample messages

Appointment reminder
Zuba: Reminder — your home inspection with [Inspector/Company] is tomorrow, [Date] at [Time], at [Address]. Questions? Call [phone]. Reply STOP to opt out, HELP for help.
Reply to HELP
Zuba appointment reminders. For help, contact support@embrallc.com. Msg & data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe.
Reply to STOP
You have been unsubscribed from Zuba appointment reminders and will receive no further messages. Reply HELP for help.

5. Opt-out & help

  • Opt out: Customers can reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe and receive no further texts.
  • Help: Customers can reply HELP, or contact support@embrallc.com .
  • Standard carrier message and data rates may apply.
  • Message frequency varies and is low (appointment reminders only).

6. Privacy

Mobile numbers are collected solely to send appointment reminders and are never sold or shared with third parties for marketing, nor shared with any party for their own marketing. Carriers (e.g., for message delivery) are not permitted to use the information for any other purpose. Full details: https://zuba-legal.pages.dev/privacy.
