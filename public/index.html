Good to know

Questions, answered.

Is Zanbi really the whole business in one app?

Yes — scheduling, a map view of your day, custom walkthrough forms, AI-assisted reports, PDF delivery, and payments all live in Zanbi. No stitching together a calendar, a maps app, a report tool, and an invoicing service.

How is it only $19.99 when other tools are $100+?

Zanbi is built lean and phone-first for independent and small-shop inspectors, not enterprise teams. You get the tools that matter without the enterprise price tag, per-report charges, or long contracts.

How does the free trial work?

You get 30 days free with no credit card required. Run real inspections, send real reports, and decide. If it's for you, it's $19.99/month after — cancel anytime.

Do I have to use my own report format?

You build it. Drag and drop sections, fields, severity scales, and photo slots to match exactly how you inspect, then reuse it on every job.

Does the AI write my report for me?

It gives you a professional first draft from your rough notes, which you review, edit, or accept. You always have the final say on every word that reaches your client.

How do payments work?

Optionally, you can invoice customers and collect payment right in the app for a 1% Zanbi platform fee. Prefer to bill your own way? Skip it — there's no extra charge.
